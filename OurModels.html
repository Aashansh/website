Aashansh at a Glance

Aashansh is an organisation with a mission to provide students with the confidence and knowledge to unlock the door to social mobility, through weekly mentorship sessions and an experiential education programme.

Through weekly mentorship and regular after school and before school center model program, we provide each student access to necessary tools like education, soft skills, micro-finance and employment.

Aashansh believes that each student needs the confidence to believe in change and the knowledge to make that change a reality. As a result, we use a two-pronged approach: Our weekly mentorship sessions nurture an unshakable sense of self-belief in each student and a daily before and after school programme establishes a strong educational foundation.

We pair each student with a mentor; a mentor who guides them, supports them and believes in them. Together, mentor and student go on a journey to unearth hidden talents, grow existing ones and build long-lasting friendships, establishing in each student the confidence that they can define their own life.

Our mentors are group of ambitious, young professionals in career paths ranging from management consultancy to graphic design to scientific research and IT. The studenrs are placed into groups of three based on their academic competency and skills and are then paired with a mentor who best suits their interest areas.

We believe however that a student needs both the confidence to believe in change and the educational foundation to make that change possible in order to truly realise their potential.

Our before and after school programme, which offers an experiential approach to learning, therefore ensures that each student has the toolkit to turn their dreams, nurtured through the mentorship sessions, into a reality.

The daily before and after school programme does not simply echo what is taught in the public school classroom. Rather, Aashansh is dedicated to creating an experiential and hands-on environment for learning in order to fully engage the imagination (and attention!) of each child. Aashansh curriculum does support however the national curriculum in order to make the learning process as productive and efficient as possible for the student.

So each student leaves Aashansh equipped with both the confidence and the knowledge required to carry on their journey towards a better life independently, and with an unshakable sense of self-worth.
